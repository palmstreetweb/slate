Spacing

Clear space & sizing

Give the lockup room — keep clear space on all sides equal to the height of the mark's red bar. Don't crowd it.

  • Min lockup width 96px
  • Min mark size 16px
  • Clear space = red-bar height
  • Wordmark font Inter ExtraBold (outlined)
  • Corner radius 22.5% of mark
